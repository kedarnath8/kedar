Light Emission and Lasing Research

Solid state lighting and displays are becoming ubiquitous in our daily lives, finding their place in televisions,cellphones,lamps and many common consumer appliances. In the opotoelectronics group, we investigate a range of polymeric and organic -inorganic hybrid materials for applications in solid-state light-emitting devices.

Organic Photovoltaics

One of the ways to satisfy the emerging need for sustainable energy sources is via the production of large-scale,cheap and easy to process solar cells. This has made the development of organic photovoltaic devices based on carbon based semiconductors an exciting and rapidly growing field of research and technology.
